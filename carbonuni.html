BitLand

Year:2022

Personal work

An interactive video installation focusing on issues of the Bitcoin industry in China's rural areas

This project focuses on social phenomena triggered by emerging technologies, with a particular attention to the Bitcoin mining industry in rural China. The work, from a personal narrative perspective, intertwines virtual digital scenes and real news images, aiming to reveal to the audience the post-modern social landscape resulting from the collision between the primitive mountain village scenery and digital society.
